Transportation Improvement Program (TIP)

The Transportation Improvement Program, or TIP, prioritizes and documents anticipated projects including roads, buses, rail, bicycle and pedestrian improvements. Each project shows a description and the expected schedule and funding. Also includes Highway Safety Improvement Program (HSIP) projects.
